Three Horizons

From first wins to enterprise scale.

  • Now 0 – 6 months

    Short-term operational lift

    • Plain-English access to GL, AP, AR, inventory, sales orders, and purchase data without opening a BI ticket.
    • Faster month-end variance research: AI explains exceptions before the controller has to chase them.
    • Vendor and customer 360s assembled on demand from FI, MM, and SD modules.
    • Reduced report queue: routine questions answered in seconds with logged, auditable lineage.
  • Next 6 – 18 months

    Medium-term depth

    • Cross-system intelligence: ERP joined with MES production data, CRM pipeline, and supply chain signals under one governance model.
    • AI-assisted analyst workflows: variance analysis, supplier risk scoring, working-capital diagnostics. Drafted by AI. Validated by humans.
    • Forecast diagnostics: AI traces forecast-actual deltas to source transactions, not just buckets.
