How We Work

Four operating principles
we never bend on.

01 · Governed Access or No Access

We never connect AI directly to raw MES tables. Every AI connection goes through a governed semantic layer. Read-only. Logged. Auditable.

02 · Built on Real Manufacturing Knowledge

The methodology is proven against real manufacturing data, not whitepapers. We don’t guess about manufacturing. We know.

03 · You Own Everything We Build

When the engagement ends, you own the code, documentation, data dictionary, and semantic layer. There is no vendor lock-in.

04 · We Report What We Find

If the audit reveals the problem is upstream of AI (process, data quality, organizational alignment), we report it clearly. We’re not here to confirm your existing plan. We’re here to give you the truth.
