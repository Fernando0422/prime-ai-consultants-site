About Prime AI

We exist because 18 years inside a real fab showed us exactly what AI gets wrong about manufacturing. And exactly how to fix it.

I spent 18 years inside Semtech’s semiconductor manufacturing operations. Not as a consultant visiting the facility. As someone who built, debugged, and improved the systems that ran production.

In that time I watched the same problem repeat itself across every organization, every team, every data initiative: AI consultants who understood AI but did not understand manufacturing. They connected AI to raw database tables. Got unreliable answers. Called it a pilot. Moved on. The manufacturer lost trust in AI.

The problem was not the technology. The problem was that the people implementing it had never been inside a fab.

Prime AI exists because I have been. For 18 years.

Our Mission

To build the AI intelligence layer that manufacturing has been waiting for. One that is governed by design, trusted by operations teams, and impossible to disconnect once it’s working.

We do not build demos. We do not build prototypes. We build infrastructure that your engineers use every day.

How We Work

Five operating principles
we never bend.

Read-Only by Default

We never write to production systems without explicit permission. Every query is logged and auditable. Your security team reviews exactly what we touched and when.

Truth Over Theater

Demos that cannot survive the next maintenance window are liabilities. We deliver working artifacts. Not slide decks.

Governed Access Always

AI models do not query raw MES tables in any Prime AI engagement. Ever. Semantic layer. Approved views. Validated query catalog. Always.

Human Authority Stays Explicit

AI suggests. Accountable owners decide. Especially on production decisions, financial data, and safety-impacting actions.

You Own the Output

When the engagement ends, everything we built belongs to you. The code. The documentation. The methodology. No lock-in. No dependency. Your call.
