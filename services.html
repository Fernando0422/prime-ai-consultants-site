Legal Disclaimer: Results & Outcomes

Prime AI provides consulting services and AI methodology. Client results depend on implementation, data quality, organizational adoption, and other factors outside Prime AI’s control. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

Prime AI does not guarantee specific business outcomes, yield improvements, decision speed increases, or other manufacturing results. Results described in case studies reflect the specific client’s situation, database architecture, and implementation approach.

AI Validation: Prime AI uses AI to identify patterns and generate hypotheses. All AI-generated insights are validated by subject matter experts before client delivery. AI is a tool that augments human judgment; it does not replace manufacturing expertise or SME validation. Clients remain responsible for all manufacturing and business decisions.

Data Access: Prime AI connects AI to read-only, approved views of your data only. All queries are logged and auditable. Prime AI does not connect AI to raw production tables. Clients are responsible for ensuring Prime AI has appropriate data access permissions and that all data handling complies with internal policies, regulatory requirements, and applicable data privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, state privacy laws, etc.).

Timeline Qualification: Prime AI’s typical engagement timelines (2 weeks for Diagnostics, 10 weeks for Build, ongoing for Retainer) are based on consulting practice experience. Actual timelines depend on schema complexity, available SME time, organizational readiness, and scope definitions. Timeline estimates will be clarified in the formal engagement proposal.
