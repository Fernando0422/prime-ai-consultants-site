Three Horizons

What changes first.What changes next.What scales.

  • Now 0 – 6 months

    Short-term operational lift

    • Natural language queries against MES data (yield trends, WIP status, equipment downtime, cycle time) without SQL or developer tickets.
    • Faster reporting: what used to take days takes minutes. Same data. No new systems.
    • Accelerated root cause analysis: AI correlates excursions, rework, tooling, and history faster than manual investigation, with receipts.
    • Reduced IT backlog: engineers answer their own exploratory questions inside governed guardrails. Developers focus on architecture, not report tickets.
  • Next 6 – 18 months

    Medium-term depth

    • AI-assisted MES development: faster prototyping of queries, APIs, and integrations with appropriate human review gates.
    • Cross-system intelligence: bridge ERP, SPC, quality, and supply chain where policy allows.
    • Conversational manufacturing analytics: “Why did yield step down on line three overnight?” Structured answers. Traceable evidence. Human validation required before action.
  • Scale 18+ months

    Long-term intelligence posture

    • Proactive operational signals alongside human authority. Predictive, not prescriptive.
    • Democratized MES data: broader conversational literacy across operations, engineering, and executive teams.
    • Multi-plant expansion: the same governed AI layer deployed across every facility.
